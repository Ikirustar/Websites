Our Story

We are problem solvers. We are the Salveo Communitas Organization.

We are a group of successful tiny home designers and builders who have come together to fight homelessness with our proud veterans.
Salveo Communitas is affordable housing, not free housing. Our veterans do not need handouts; they need housing that is high value, offers community, and is sustainably priced. Sustainable means the community, as a whole, generates enough rental income for the homes and common areas to be well maintained, keeping the entire community a beautiful and welcoming place for all our Villagers.
Each self contained studio apartment consists of a kitchen, bathroom, sofa with built-in full size bed, TV, built-in shelves, and an expanding porch area.
