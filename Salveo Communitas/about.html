About Us

Our Mission

Our plans are to get commitments to support 120 Bungalows at this first site. We want to create a welcoming, beautiful community for our veterans where they have access to quality living spaces.

How we Got Started

After coming across a fellow Army Veteran at a coffee shop, Ben discovered the vetreran was homeless and had been homeless for quite some time. He opened up on his challenges and what led him to becoming homeless; Medical issues that wiped out his savings and no family, which all contributed to not being able to make enough to afford his own place. There is a shortage of affordable housing in this city and it is squeezing everyone out of a place to live. That's why we're passionate about creating small footprint houses that anyone can afford.
