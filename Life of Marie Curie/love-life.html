Relationship with Pierre

Pierre Curie Initially gave up on love. He believed romance would interrupt work. However, when he met Marie, he begged her to stay in Paris to marry him. They both shared their love for science and they embarked on it together. Pierre was more joyful than Marie. Marie had a seriousness about her, and Pierre's attitude sort of complimented that. Marie had a difficult childhood, and having Pierre alleviated that.

After the accidental death of Pierre Curie, Marrie Currie was torn. She struggled on one hand to find a reason to live. But is was also a challenge putting her life back together with her kids. There was eventually a cheating scandal that she got herself involved in. but she ended up winning two nobel prizes at the time. Despite being discouraged to collect them, she courageously showed up and collected it herself.
